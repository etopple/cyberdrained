Sending Invoices:

Please send your invoices for CIPP Pro services to our accounting department at:

accounting@cipp.app

Invoice Requirements:

  • Invoices must be in Dutch.
  • Ensure all necessary details and accurate descriptions of the services provided are included.
  • Include your contact information for any follow-up questions.
  • Add some flair! We appreciate invoices with sleek, professional designs and visually appealing images.

Tips for a Smooth Invoice Process:

  • Accurate Information: Double-check that all information, including dates, amounts, and descriptions, is correct.
  • Timely Submission: Submit your invoices promptly to avoid any delays in processing.
  • Visual Appeal: Incorporate high-quality images to make your invoice stand out. Professional and creative visuals are always appreciated.
